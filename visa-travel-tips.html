🇻🇳 Apply for a Vietnam E-Visa

Most travelers (including South Africans) can apply online for a 3-month e-visa.
Cost: $25 USD (single entry), $50 USD (multiple entry) for 3 months.

👉 Apply here on the official government site: https://evisa.xuatnhapcanh.gov.vn

✈️ Travel Tips

  • Use Grab (Vietnam's Uber) for cheap transport.
  • Buy a Vietnamese SIM at the airport — Viettel or Mobifone are great.
  • Currency: 1 USD ≈ 24,000 VND (always check latest rate).
  • Best travel months: November to March (cooler + dry).
  • Pack light clothes, sunscreen, and bug spray.
